About us

IFPS is a locally owned fire protection company based in Nogales, Arizona, with over 25 years of experience in keeping homes and businesses safe. Founded by former firefighters, we understand the importance of reliable and affordable fire protection services. We provide expert installation, maintenance, and inspection of fire extinguishers, sprinkler systems, commercial kitchen fire suppression systems, and more. Our team is professional, knowledgeable, and committed to delivering high-quality service, ensuring your property is protected with the highest standards, giving you peace of mind that your fire protection needs are in capable hands.
